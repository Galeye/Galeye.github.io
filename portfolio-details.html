About Project

Musician is an app specifically designed to reduce the workload of people who are getting married. Moreover, it is to let them find the right musicians for them easily and quickly.

Musician app for a Wedding Ceremony

  • 01 PROJECT OVERVIEW

    The Problem
    People who are getting married face trouble in finding the right musician/s for their wedding ceremony in a given short period of time.

    The Goal
    Design a musician booking app for people getting married so that they can easily find their choice of musicians.

    My role
    Design a musician booking app for people getting married so that they can easily find their choice of musicians.

    Target Audience
    People who are getting married.

  • 02 Understanding the User

    User Research:Summary
    I conducted interviews and created empathy maps to better understand the users I am designing for and their needs. A primary user group identified through research were adults who are planning to get married and want an easy way to find musician for their wedding ceremony.

    User Research:Pain Points

    iTime: Adults who are getting married have to make lots of preparations within short amount of time.

    iiAccessibility: Platforms for booking musicians are not equipped with assistive technologies.

    iiiNo proper platform: There are not many musician booking app so it is harder for people to find musicians

    ivLocation:There are not many musician booking app so it is harder for people to find musicians.

  • 03Accessibility Considerations

    iUsed icons to make navigation easier.

    iiUsed images of musicians to help all users better understand the designs.

    iiiProvided access to users who are vision impaired through adding alt text to images for screen readers.

Challenges

It was challenging to find people who were getting married for my usability study.

Take away

This app makes users feel like musicians app really thinks about how to meet the right musicians.

What I learned

The first ideas for the app are only beginning of the process. If we take feedback positively, it only helps in enhancing our work.

Next Steps

Add more features and make the app more fun to use it. Also,Conduct more user research to determine any new areas of need.
