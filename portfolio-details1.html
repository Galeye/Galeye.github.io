About Project

Dog breed identification website is a website designed specifically for people who wants to know the breed of a dog.

Dog Breed Identification Website

  • 01 PROJECT OVERVIEW

    The Problem
    Most types of dogs look similar but are of different breeds. Knowing dog's breed is not trivial, as different breeds were evolved to perform different tasks. Some breeds, for instance, were bred to hunt, kill and more likely to bite. Unfortunately, humans have hard time telling them apart, until the dog comes from one of the few popular and distinctive breeds such as golden retriever.

    The Goal
    Design a dog breed identification website to identify the breed of a dog

    My role
    Design a dog breed identification website to identify the breed of a dog

    Target Audience
    People who want to know the breed of a dog.

  • 02 Understanding the User

    User Research:Summary
    I conducted interviews and created empathy maps to better understand the users I am designing for and their needs. A primary user group identified through research were people who are interested in knowing the breed of a dog.

    User Research:Pain Points

    iTime: People who wants to know the breed of a dog can't go to DNA experts everytime.

    iiAccessibility: Dog experts are not readily available

    iiiNo proper platform: There are not many Dog breed identification website so it is harder for people to know the breed of a dog.

    ivCost:It is costly for users to perfom DNA test everytime they want to know the breed of a dog.

  • 03Accessibility Considerations

    iUsed icons to make navigation easier.

    iiUsed images of Dogs to help all users better understand the designs.

    iiiProvided access to users who are vision impaired through adding alt text to images for screen readers.

Challenges

It was challenging to integrate website with deep learning.

Take away

This app makes users feel closer to dogs.

What I learned

The first ideas for the website are only beginning of the process. If we take feedback positively, it only helps in enhancing our work.

Next Steps

Add more features and make the website more fun to use it. Also,Conduct more user research to determine any new areas of need.
